    A: To maintain a healthy diet, include a variety of fruits, vegetables, lean proteins, and whole grains in your meals.

  • Q: What are the common symptoms of the flu?

    A: Common symptoms of the flu include fever, cough, sore throat, body aches, and fatigue.

    A: You should see a doctor if you have persistent or severe symptoms, or if you're unsure about a medical issue.
